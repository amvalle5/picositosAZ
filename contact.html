Product Information

What are dulces enchilados?

What are dulces enchilados?

Dulces enchilados are traditional Mexican candies coated in a spicy-sweet chili blend, creating a perfect balance of sweet, sour, and heat.

How spicy are they?

They do have spicy taste to them but they're not super spicy, they have more of a sweet, sour, salty taste to them.

We currently do no offer sugar-free or vegan options.
