Product Information

Dulces enchilados are traditional candies coated in a flavorful chili blend, offering a unique mix of sweet, sour, and spicy tastes.

They have a mild to moderate spice level, balanced by sweet, sour, and salty flavors. Most people find them enjoyable rather than overwhelmingly spicy.

At this time, we do not offer sugar-free or vegan options. We hope to expand our offerings in the future.
